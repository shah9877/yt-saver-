Supported Video Formats and Quality Options

YTSaver supports downloading YouTube videos in MP4 format, which is the most universally compatible video format available. MP4 files play natively on all modern devices including Windows PCs, Macs, iPhones, Android phones, smart TVs, and tablets — without requiring any additional software or codecs.

Depending on the original upload quality of the video, you may find options ranging from 144p up to 4K (2160p). For most purposes, we recommend downloading in 1080p Full HD for the best balance of visual quality and file size. If you have limited storage or a slower internet connection, 720p HD is an excellent choice that still looks sharp on most screens.

Is It Legal to Download YouTube Videos?

This is a common question with an important nuance. YouTube's Terms of Service technically prohibit downloading videos without explicit permission from the platform or content creator. However, downloading videos for personal, offline, non-commercial use exists in a legal grey area in many jurisdictions. Many countries recognize the concept of "private copying" or "fair use," which may permit downloading content you have already legally accessed.

As a general rule: only download videos that you have the right to access, never re-upload downloaded content without permission, and do not use downloaded videos for commercial purposes. When in doubt, check the specific copyright laws applicable in your country.
